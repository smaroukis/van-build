Over two years my partner and I took a bare metal cargo van and converted it to an off-grid solar and battery powered camper van.

The project forced me to get hands on and learn new skills in design, 3D modeling, solar and battery systems, woodworking, plumbing, welding and more.

You can start at About or see the page below for overviews of the systems. For the truly curious check out Behind The Scenes for even more build details.
